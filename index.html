SDG Connect

Despite the progress that has been made towards achieving equal rights for both men and women, considerable work still remains to be done in the UK.
SDG Connect aims to connect those that have suffered from causes of gender inequality to charities within the UK that are working to support those causes

Aim

SDG Connect is a charitable organisation that aims to empower the lives of women through education. We aim to do this through connecting people to organisations that directly tackle community issues.
