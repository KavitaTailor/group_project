Our Causes



Pay Women Equally

SDG connect is an advocate for issues that span across both the global north and the global south of the continent.

It is imperative to us and all of our team to make sure that women are treated as equals, especially in the workplace. Women have been systemically disadvantaged in this way, and we know how it is important for us to do advocate for these causes.

End Violence Against Women

Violence against women is a painful global epidemic that has taken multiple forms. Sexual violence, especially is what we specialise in, and we do not want women to live in a world where they are fearful for their safety, or of their bodily autonomy being violated.

Our team aim to educate all groups of people on the community as well as the global impact this has.
